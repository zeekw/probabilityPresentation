How does it work?

The game is simple. All you need is an old turntable, styrofoam, PVC piping, and some darts.
The objective of the game is to score doubles.

Preparation

Create a target divided into 6 equally sized segments.

How to play

Close your eyes, and while the target is spinning drop the dart town the tube and onto the target.
